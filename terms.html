1. Overview

ShopX is committed to providing a transparent and reliable shopping experience. This policy explains how orders are processed, shipped, and delivered.

Orders are verified for stock, payment, and authenticity before dispatch. Processing usually takes 3–7 working days.

Orders are shipped via trusted courier partners across India. Delivery timelines vary based on location and logistics availability.

4. Tracking

Once shipped, tracking details will be shared via email or SMS.

5. Delays

Delays may occur due to weather, courier issues, or unforeseen events. ShopX is not liable for delays after dispatch.

6. Damaged Products

Report damaged or defective products within 48 hours with proof. Eligible cases may receive replacement, refund, or store credit.

7. Returns & Refunds

Returns are accepted only for damaged, defective, or incorrect items.

8. Customer Responsibility

Customers must provide accurate delivery details. Failed deliveries due to incorrect info are not the responsibility of ShopX.
